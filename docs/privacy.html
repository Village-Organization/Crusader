Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2025

Overview

Crusader is a desktop and mobile email client built by Village Organization. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Crusader accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

When you connect an email account, Crusader requests access to the following through OAuth 2.0:

  • Email address and profile information — to identify your account
  • Email messages — to display, search, and manage your inbox
  • Send email — to send messages on your behalf when you compose and send

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your emails are fetched via IMAP and cached locally on your device in a SQLite database for offline access and fast search.
  • Emails are sent via SMTP using your authenticated account.
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage.
  • No email content, credentials, or personal data is transmitted to our servers. Crusader communicates directly with your email provider (Gmail, Outlook).

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your operating system's secure credential store (Windows Credential Manager, iOS Keychain).
  • Cached emails are stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • We do not operate any servers that store your data.

Third-Party Services

Crusader connects to the following third-party services:

  • Google (Gmail) — for OAuth authentication and email access via IMAP/SMTP. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft (Outlook) — for OAuth authentication and email access via IMAP/SMTP. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • Gravatar — to fetch contact avatars based on email address hashes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstalling Crusader removes all locally cached data.
  • You can disconnect your account at any time from Settings, which removes stored tokens.
  • You can revoke Crusader's access from your Google Account permissions or Microsoft Account permissions at any time.

Children's Privacy

Crusader is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
