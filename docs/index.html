Crusader

A sleek, modern email client that finally feels good to use. Glassmorphism design, keyboard-first navigation, zero bloat.

Features

Unified Inbox

Connect Gmail and Outlook accounts. All your email in one place with threaded conversations.

Keyboard-First

Vim-style shortcuts inspired by Superhuman. Navigate, reply, archive, and search without touching your mouse.

Offline-Ready

Local SQLite cache means instant reads. Background sync keeps everything up to date.

Beautiful Design

Dark mode with glassmorphism, neon accents, 12 color themes, and 18 font choices.

Rich Compose

Full rich text editor, file attachments, Undo Send, and per-account signatures.

Cross-Platform

Built with Flutter. Available on Windows and iOS.
